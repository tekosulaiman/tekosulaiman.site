Analysis

This page highlights my approach as a System Analyst (SA) and Business Analyst (BA) in translating business needs into structured technical solutions. My focus is on understanding context, breaking down complexity, and producing documentation that can be directly used by both developers and stakeholders.

  • Business needs observation: from client requests to identifying core features.
  • System breakdown and gap analysis: comparing current conditions with the ideal solution.
  • Analytical documentation: flowcharts, BRD, FSD, and clear cross-team communication.

The articles below are based on direct exploration and practice, not theory. They are written to help both technical and non-technical teams understand how structured analysis can accelerate decision-making and strengthen product execution.
